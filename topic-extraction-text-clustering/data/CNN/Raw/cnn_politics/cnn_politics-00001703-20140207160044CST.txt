<TITLE>Man apprehended trying to jump White House fence</TITLE>
<TIME>Fri, 07 Feb 2014 17:00:44 EST</TIME>
<ABSTRACT>
A man with two bags tried to jump the fence outside the White House on Friday and was apprehended, a Secret Service spokesman told CNN.
</ABSTRACT>
<TEXT>
Washington (CNN) -- A man tried to jump the White House fence on Friday, causing a brief lockdown while he was apprehended, law enforcement officials said.
Police declared the incident over after checking a briefcase and taking the man into custody. He was not identified.
President Barack Obama was not at the White House at the time. He was in Michigan for a speech and the signing of the farm bill.
