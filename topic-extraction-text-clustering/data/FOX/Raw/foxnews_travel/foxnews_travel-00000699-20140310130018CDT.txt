James Bond’s biggest fans have finally found a place to celebrate their movie hero in style. The 007 Suite in Seven Hotel Paris is outfitted with everything you need to feel like the world’s most dashing spy.
The suite features golden guns, a mirrored ceiling above the bed, a golden-accented toilet and a signature gun-toting Connery wall mural. Fans can watch the entire catalog of 007 films from the comfort of their bedroom in a big screen TV.
Located within the Seven Hotel in Paris, France, the suite is popular with Bond fans from around the world, as well as tourists looking to add a bit of mystery to their holiday, The Daily Mail reports.
The hotel website boasts that “you’ll never want to leave” this suite with its “exceptional decor in a mythical atmosphere, a blend of vintage chic and futuristic design. Lap up the sophistication of the suite for the duration of your stay, wallowing in its subdued elegance, for an unforgettable taste of adventure.”
The Seven hotel also offers different themed rooms, including a Marie Antoinette Suite and a Black Diamond room complete with Swarovski crystal bathtub and basin
For international spy enthusiasts who need a sophisticated place to rest, the suite starts at €447 a night, which is about $620.
